Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I save compared to new cabinets? +
Most customers save 60-80% compared to full cabinet replacement. Instead of spending $20,000-40,000+ on new cabinets, refinishing typically costs a fraction while achieving beautiful results.
How long does the process take? +
The typical project takes 1-2 weeks from pickup to delivery, depending on the size and complexity of your kitchen. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.
What condition do my cabinets need to be in? +
We can refinish most cabinet doors and drawer fronts as long as they're structurally sound. Even cabinets with wear, scratches, or outdated finishes can be transformed into beautiful, like-new pieces.
Do you provide the paint and materials? +
Yes! Paint Magic supplies all paint, materials, and instructions needed for both the professional spray work and the trim painting you'll do at home. Everything is included in your quote.
What if I'm not satisfied with the results? +
We guarantee your satisfaction! With over 21 years of experience and hundreds of happy customers, we stand behind our work. We won't consider the job complete until you're thrilled with the results.
